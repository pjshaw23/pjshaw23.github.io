For our AENG 110 Video project, my group had a ton of fun making this. It was a serious video about a building we love but we had fun making and editing it. It took us about an entire period to film all that we needed for our video. Our video was about the Osburn building and the different areas/shops in the building.

We started the video by filming an intro to walking into the building and showing the first-floor shops, metals, woods, polymers, etc. then moved to the top floor, where it is a lot of computer labs and the OSHA section. Lastly, we moved downstairs to film the lounge, and a lot of the trophies that our clubs have won over the years. We used IMovie for the editing and we had a lot of fun doing it because there is a lot of options you have with it and I thought we had a good looking funny video and we are proud of it.
